    Durian

    Automatically scan for Path of Exile items that suites your price. If an item matches your parameters, you'll get a notification.

    Durian Demo

    Download

    Download the Durian-<version>.jar file. You'll need at least Java 8 runtime installed. To run: just double click on the jar file.
